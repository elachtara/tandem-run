How it works (it's easy)

Build your profile

Neighborhood, pace, time of day, overall vibe. These are the details that will help us pick your perfect match.

Verify your identity

All members are required to verify their identity through the Persona ID verification flow. We verify identities, but do not conduct background checks.

Meet your match

We match you with one other highly compatible woman in your area. We supply one of our hand-curated routes.

Make it happen

Propose a time to meet up in the app and then she accepts. Simple as that! No texting required.

By women, for women

Hi bestie!

We're Meg and Em. We built Tandem Run because every woman deserves a buddy.

Run clubs are great, and we love Strava, but neither helps you find that girl who is running at the same time, same pace, and same routes as you.

We've felt that frustration, so we decided to build the thing we wished existed: a simple way to find a buddy nearby. Tandem Run matches you with a partner near you who moves at a similar pace and time as you, so you don't have to compromise your schedule.

Whether you just moved to Boston and don't know a soul, or you've lived here your whole life, we want to help you find your person. Because the city feels a little more like home, when you've got someone beside you :)

Women only by design

Tandem Run is open to all who identify as women, including transgender women and nonbinary individuals who feel comfortable in a women-centered space.

Designed with safety in mind

We've thought carefully about safety from day one. Identity is verified through Persona, and we suggest meeting points in public, well-trafficked areas.

Inclusive of all abilities

Everyone is welcome, at any ability level, including guide running, wheelchair users, stroller-pushers and anyone who moves differently.
