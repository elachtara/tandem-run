The problem

Running alone
shouldn't feel like
a risk.

01

Run clubs are too noisy

30 people, mixed paces, social chaos. You spend more time making small talk and catching your breath than actually enjoying the run.

02

Strava doesn't make plans

Strava is a highlight reel, not a calendar. You can cheer someone's Sunday long run. You just can't be on it.

03

Texting friends doesn't scale

Your friends have different schedules, different paces, different goals. Finding the overlap every week is exhausting.

04

First runs are awkward

Meeting a stranger for an hour-long run is a real commitment. Most people don't bother — so they just run alone.

How it works

From profile
to running together
in minutes.

Built around one action: proposing a specific run. No swiping, no chasing. Just two runners who fit, agreeing to show up.

01

Build your profile

Pace range, preferred run types, when you're free, how chatty you like to be. The details that actually matter.

02

Get matched

We surface one or two highly compatible runners near you. Pace, schedule, consistency, and vibe — all considered.

03

Propose a run

Pick a day, distance, route, and effort level. Send it. They accept, tweak, or suggest another time. Done.

04

Build a routine

After a good run, reschedule with one tap. The best partnerships become weekly habits — and that's the whole point.

Why Tandem Run

Built by women.
For women who want to
run without thinking twice.

Women only — by design

Every profile on Tandem Run is verified as a woman. This isn't a filter or a setting — it's the foundation. We built it this way from day one because we believe you should never have to think twice about who you're meeting.

Built by women

Tandem Run was founded and built by women who run. We've felt the mental load of solo running — the routes we avoid, the hours we skip, the headphones we leave out just in case. We built the app we wished existed.

Safety first

Live location sharing, check-in system, verified profiles, and suggested meeting points in public, well-lit areas. Every feature was designed with your safety as the starting point, not an afterthought.

Precision matching

Not just pace — we match on run type, schedule, social preference, and consistency level. The difference between a great partner and an awkward one is in the details.

Truly accessible

Built for guide running, wheelchair users, mixed-ability pairs, and anyone who moves differently. You set your pace — we find someone who matches it. No ability level required, no explanation needed.

More than miles

Some find a training partner. Some find a friend. Running together consistently creates real bonds — the kind that form when you show up for each other, week after week, in all weather.
