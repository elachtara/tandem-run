From profile
to running together
in minutes.

No more "we should run sometime" or "just come to my run club." You propose a real run — day, distance, route — she says yes, tweaks it, or suggests another time. It's that easy.

Build your profile

Pace range, preferred run types, neighborhood, when you're free, how chatty you like to be.

Meet your match

We suggest one or two highly compatible runners near you.

Make it happen

Pick a day, distance, route, and effort level — then send it. They accept, tweak, or suggest another time.

Become regulars

After a good run, reschedule with one tap. There is nothing better than a weekly ritual with your running buddy.

Built by women,
for women.

Women only — by design

Every profile is verified. We built it this way from day one because we believe you should never have to think twice about who you're meeting. Tandem Run is open to all who identify as women, including transgender women and nonbinary individuals who feel comfortable in a women-centered space.

Built by women

Tandem Run was built by women who run. We've felt the mental weight of running solo and appreciated the freedom of running with a partner. We built the app we wished existed.

Safety first

Live location sharing, check-in system, verified profiles, and suggested public meeting points. Every feature was designed with your safety in mind.

Precision matching

Not just pace — we match on run type, schedule, social preference, and consistency level. The difference between a great partner and an awkward one is in the details.

Truly accessible

Built for guide running, wheelchair users, mixed-ability pairs, and anyone who moves differently. Everyone is welcome, at any ability level.
